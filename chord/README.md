# Chord — Team Resonance

**By Angelo & Liz**

A visual Enneagram tool for understanding team dynamics. Not a quiz. Not a forecast. A persistent map of how people relate.

## What It Does

- Add team members with their Enneagram type (1–9)
- Click cards to toggle people on/off
- The Enneagram diagram lights up showing connections between active types
- A reading appears describing the dynamics — center balance, pair dynamics, gaps

## The Concept

Enneagram describes fixed personality architecture — not daily moods, but deep structural patterns. Chord makes those patterns visible when you arrange them in a team context.

Click two people on: see the specific tension or harmony between those types.  
Click a full team on: see what the group's center of gravity is, and what's missing.

## Running It

Just open `index.html` in a browser. No build step, no server needed.

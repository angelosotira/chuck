How We Work

Our process is as considered
as our craft.

01
Pre-Construction

Before a single beam is placed, we know every constraint, every cost, every risk. Our pre-construction team builds the project twice — first on paper, then in steel and concrete. Surprises are for birthdays, not building sites.

02
Construction

We hire and manage the best tradespeople in every market we operate in. Our superintendents have been with KADE an average of 11 years. When we say we'll deliver on time, we mean it — because we've built the schedule we're promising.

Delivery is not the end of our responsibility. Every KADE project comes with a 2-year warranty on all work performed and a dedicated project manager for post-completion issues. We're in this for the long relationship, not the last punch list item.

The Katz Family

Built for Griffin and Hayley — and everyone who comes after them.

KADE was named for two people before anyone else: Griffin and Hayley. Everything we build is meant to still be standing when they're old enough to understand why.

The company was founded in 2008 by the Katz family — second-generation builders whose father laid the foundations of their craft on the construction sites of mid-century New York. The griffin, guardian of built structures in classical architecture since ancient Greece, became our mark. It's Griffin's name, and it's the oldest symbol of permanent construction in the world. That's not a coincidence. That's intention.

Hayley's name comes from the old English "hale" — robust, enduring — and "ley," the ground you build on. She's in the tagline too, even if she doesn't know it yet: built for those who come after.

Today, KADE operates across New York, Los Angeles, and Miami with a team of 340 professionals. We build offices, residences, cultural institutions, and hospitality environments that are meant to outlast the trends of their era. We don't build the most projects. We build the right ones — for the right clients, with the right intentions — and we build them to last.

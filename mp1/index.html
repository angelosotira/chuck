How It Works

Discover

Browse our curated collection of generative works. Every piece is hand-selected by our curation board — artists who have defined the medium.

Acquire

Your first acquisition includes your Layer Canvas — the only display built for this art. Existing members add works to their collection at the art price alone.

Live With

Art Intelligence schedules your collection across the day. Your artists earn ViewTime royalties while their work fills your space. The art evolves. So do you.

ViewTime Royalties

Artists earn every hour their work is on your wall.

ViewTime is Layer's royalty engine. Every canvas reports display time in real time. Artists receive payment proportional to how long their work is shown — not just when it's sold. Acquire a work, and you become a patron of the artist's ongoing practice.

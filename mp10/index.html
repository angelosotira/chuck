How It Works
01 — Select
Choose a work
Browse the collection. Select a work for the person and the space you have in mind. We'll help if you need guidance.
02 — Personalize
Write your message
A gift message accompanies the provenance certificate. It becomes part of the work's permanent record.
03 — Deliver
Canvas or digital reveal
If the recipient owns a Layer Canvas, the work is added to their collection immediately. Otherwise, a Cosmos display arrives with the gift.
04 — Earn
The artist earns
From the moment the work begins playing, ViewTime royalties flow to the artist. Your gift is an act of patronage — ongoing, not once.

ViewTime

ViewTime is Layer's proprietary royalty model. Artists are compensated in direct proportion to display time: every hour a work runs on a Canvas, the artist earns. There is no equivalent mechanism in the traditional art market. Acquisition is not the end of the relationship between artist and collector — it is the beginning.

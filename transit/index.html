How It Works

01 — Your Birth Chart

A snapshot of the sky

Enter your birth date, time, and city. The system calculates the exact position of eight celestial bodies at that moment — the same data an astrologer uses to cast your natal chart.

02 — Today's Weather

The sky right now

Every time you load the piece, it recalculates the current positions of the same eight bodies. The angular relationship between now and your birth creates that day's astrological "weather."

03 — A Living Image

No two are the same

The combined data drives a generative WebGL2 shader. Your birth signature sets the color palette and base structure. Daily transits modulate the intensity of each axis. The result is yours alone — and different every day.

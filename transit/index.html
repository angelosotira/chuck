The Concept

Transit is named for the astrological term: the ongoing movement of planets across the sky and their relationship to fixed points in your natal chart. Every day, the planetary weather shifts. Every person's natal chart is different. The intersection — your birth sky against today's sky — is what each work in this series makes visible.

Each iteration explores a different generative system for that data. The underlying engine stays constant. The visual physics evolves.

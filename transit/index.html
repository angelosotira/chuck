How It Works

01 — Your Natal Chart

Your Natal Chart

Enter your birth date, time, and location. We calculate the exact position of the Sun, Moon, Mercury, Venus, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, and Uranus at the moment you were born.

02 — Today's Transits

Today's Transits

Each day, the planets move. We compare where they are now against where they were at your birth — producing eight unique resonance scores.

03 — The Nebula

The Nebula

Those scores become color weights in a WebGL2 fragment shader. The swirling form is pure organic noise — only the palette reflects you, recalculated every sunrise.

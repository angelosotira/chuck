How It Works

01 — Your Birth Chart

A snapshot of the sky

Enter your birth date, time, and city. Eight planetary positions are calculated for that exact moment — the same data an astrologer uses to read your natal chart.

02 — Today's Weather

The sky right now

Every time the piece loads, it recalculates the current positions of those same eight bodies. The angular relationship between then and now creates that day's astrological weather.

03 — A Living Image

Uniquely yours, daily

The combined data drives a generative WebGL2 shader. Your birth signature sets the palette and structure. Daily transits modulate it. The result is yours alone — and different every day.
